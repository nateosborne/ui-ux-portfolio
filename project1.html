Circle City Spots

Problem Space:

It can be difficult to find quality places to shop for clothing items, and even if a place is found, they may not have what you like or want.

Solution:

Create a website that displays nearby outlets and items, which also allows users to show off new findings, as well as the selections for that day or week.

My role:

  • UI & UX Designer
  • UX Researcher

PERSONA:

Design Approach

Utlizing user-centered design, I created this website with the user's wants and needs in mind.

Ideas for Features:

  • Lists and categories of clothing items and stores
  • Store map
  • Forums for users to post and discuss findings
  • User accounts
