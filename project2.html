Virtual Photography

Problem Space:

One of my favorite hobbies is playing video games, and I love taking photos of different landscapes and objects within those games.

Solution:

I wanted a place to showcase those works, so I decided to create a gallery website for all of my favorite photographs.

My role:

  • UI & UX Designer
  • UX Researcher

Design Approach

After looking through some other photography portfolios online, I took some inspiration and decided to create a minimalist gallery website.

Ideas for Features:

  • Gallery of featured photographs on home page
  • Clickable images to expand photographs
  • Image carousel to choose between categories
  • About & Contact page
